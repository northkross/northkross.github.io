The Art of Speedcubing

About Speedcubing

Speedcubing is the practice of solving a Rubik's Cube as quickly as possible. It has evolved into a competitive sport with world records being broken regularly. Enthusiasts around the globe participate in competitions, sharing techniques and pushing the boundaries of human dexterity and memory.
