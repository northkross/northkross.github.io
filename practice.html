    Benchmark Timer

    00:00.00

    The benchmark is a different form of practice aimed at your ability to form muscle memory when reading scrambles and algorithms. Start the timer and do the scramble. When you complete it, stop the timer and record your time. Doing this daily will make your learning process with advanced methods faster and make scrambling cubes faster as well. It will also help beginners learn cube notation quickly.
